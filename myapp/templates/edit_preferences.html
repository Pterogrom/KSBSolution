Volatility Calculation

This function calculates the volatility based on historical data, allowing you to analyze the risk and potential return of your selected options.

Beta Calculation

Beta is a measure of a stock's volatility in relation to the overall market. This function allows you to calculate the Beta value for your selected stock or sector.

Sharpe Ratio Calculation

The Sharpe ratio is used to evaluate the risk-adjusted return of an investment. It helps compare different investments' performance adjusted for risk.

VaR (Value at Risk) Calculation

VaR is a measure used to assess the potential loss in the value of an investment portfolio due to market movements. It estimates the worst loss under normal market conditions.

Drawdown Calculation

Drawdown refers to the decline from a historical peak in some variable (such as portfolio value). It is an indicator of downside risk.
